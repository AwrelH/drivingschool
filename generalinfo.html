The basics from zero to 100

This is the roadmap you will have to take to get your license.

Get a learner's permit

You need first this no matter what vehicle you intend on driving (car, truck, motorbike)! Follow the instructions on how to get a driving permit (called a ’körkortstillstånd’ in Swedish).

Prepare for your driving exam

Everybody needs to practice! It is important to remember that you might be an excellent driver back in your own country, driving in Sweden can be very different, and it's important to feel confident and safe before going for your driving test. We will help you reach that point.

Prepare for your theory exam

Driving theory is very important, particularly with all the different Swedish road signs around! Thankfully there are plenty of ways to practice theory leading up to your tests. On our homepage you will be available to do online-test you prepare you for the actual test. There is also multiple education videos to help you drill those more difficult sections.

Take your Risk 1 and 2 courses

These courses are mandatory! It's a general hazard risks in traffic and driving under hazardous circumstances, like rain, break-test. Risk 2 you get to drive on a slippery road. There's only a few facilities that provide this course.

Take your Driving exams (theory and driving)

This is your final step to obtain your Swedish Driver's License! These tests are both taken at Trafikverket Förarprov. You can either book the tests yourself or we will help you with it. Our students pass their first theory exam with a 90% rate.
